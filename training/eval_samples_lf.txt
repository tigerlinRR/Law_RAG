ITEM 2.01
COMPLETION OF ACQUISITION OR DISPOSITION OF ASSETS

On February 27, 2026, Knightscope, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the “Company”), completed the acquisition of all of the issued and outstanding membership interests of Event Risk LLC, an Indiana limited liability company (the “Acquired Business”), from Eric Rose (the “Seller”), pursuant to that certain Securities Purchase Agreement, dated February 27, 2026 (the “Purchase Agreement”), by and among the Company, the Company, and the Seller.

Pursuant to the Purchase Agreement, the aggregate consideration paid by the Company to the Seller for the Acquired Business consisted of:

1.  A cash payment of $5,000,000;
2.  The issuance of 1,724,418 shares of the Company’s Class A Common Stock, par value $0.001 per share;
3.  Deferred cash payments totaling $4,000,000, payable in quarterly installments of $500,000 beginning March 31, 2027 and ending December 31, 2028;
4.  Potential earn-out payments;
5.  Potential cash revenue share payments; and
6.  Potential equity revenue share issuances.

Additionally, prior to or simultaneously with the closing, the Acquired Business distributed $663,045.09 in cash to the Seller. The Company also discharged certain indebtedness of the Acquired Business, including amounts owed to Frost Bank. The purchase price is subject to adjustment based on the final determination of net working capital and company expenses as of the closing date.

The foregoing description of the Purchase Agreement does not purport to be complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Purchase Agreement, filed as Exhibit 2.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K, which is incorporated herein by reference.
